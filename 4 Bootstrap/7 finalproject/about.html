About Us

Mission

Our mission is to create a welcoming enviroment so that all cheerleaders can learn more about the sport that means the world to them.

Vision

We envision cheerleaders coming from all over, meeting so many new people, creating a fun and loving enviroment, and it all being based around the sport Cheerleading.

Goals

  • Help new cheerleaders learn about scoring, different levels, and new skills.
  • Help experienced highschool cheerleaders learn how to get into recruiting and the college cheer world

History

The National Cheer Conference first started in 2015. It was very small and not a lot of people new about it. We had very limited speakers and it didn't make much money. Our owner though, never gave up and by 2019 it was a flourishing conference with thousands of participants every year. We have grown from the ground up and can't wait to grow even more!

Achieved

  • In 2018 we earned the Largest Growing Conference Award
  • In 2023 we became the largest cheer conference in the world

Notable Events

Every year is amazing but our most notable event was the 2024 conference where we had over 10,000 athletes come to our conference. We broke our record in many things. Most scholarships given out, most guest speakers, most rigourous schedule, and most parents finally understanding the hype of cheer!
