National Cheer Conference 2025

Welcome everybody to the Annual National Cheer Conference 2025!

  • This event is a cheer based conference focused on bringing in the cheer community for a fun but informational time!
  • This event takes place on June 21st, 2025.
  • There will be many speakers there who are very experienced and are some fan favorites in the cheer world!

Host

The organization hosting and organizing this event is USASF. This is a very successful organization in the cheer world.

Key Features and Attractions

  • Six speakers fron the staff of USASF
  • Three guest Speakers
  • College scholarship and recruiting table
  • There will be fun games and snacks too!

We are so excited to see everyone there!
