My Favorite Hobby


Intro

  • My favorite hobby is coloring.
  • Outside of cheer and school, I enjoy coloring books and color by number games on my phone.
  • I feel that coloring helps me feel less stressed and helps me get my mind off of things.
  • I have multiple adult coloring books in my house and I also have many color by number games on my phone and ipad.

Coloring Books

When I am bored at home I love to use colored pencils and color in a coloring book. I also color with markers
sometimes but I prefer colored pencils. I like the adult coloring books with intricate designs. Sometimes though,
I do enjoy simple drawings in kids coloring books. Coloring books help me feel calm and forget about anything
that I am stressed about. I've always liked coloring books but I just recently started using them more. My favorite
designs to color are plants, hearts, landscapes,and things like that.
