Description- Physical therapists are healthcare professionals who specialize in evaluating, diagnosing, and treating movement disorders and musculoskeletal injuries. Their responsibilities include assessment and diagnosis, treatment planning, intervention and rehabilitation, patient education, and collaborating with others.
Average Salary for a PT- Median wage: $99,710, lowest 10% earn less than: $72,260, highest 10% earn more than: $130,870.
