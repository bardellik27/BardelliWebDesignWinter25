Physical Therapist

Description

Physical therapists are healthcare professionals who specialize in evaluating, diagnosing, and treating movement disorders and musculoskeletal injuries. Their responsibilities include assessment and diagnosis, treatment planning, intervention and rehabilitation, patient education, and collaborating with others.

Average Salary

Median wage: $99,710, lowest 10% earn less than: $72,260, highest 10% earn more than: $130,870.

Employers

  • Offices of Other Health Practitioners
  • General Medical and Surgical Hospitals
  • Home Health Care Services
  • Offices of Physicians
  • Nursing Care Facilities (Skilled Nursing Facilities)

Related Careers

  • Occupational Therapists: specializes in helping people improve their ability to perform daily activities, known as "occupations."
  • Clinical Nurse Specialists: ensure adherence to established clinical policies, protocols, regulations, and standards.
  • Nurse Practitioners: diagnose and treat acute, episodic, or chronic illness, independently or as part of a healthcare team
